About Spectra

Built for PNG.
Backed by Experience.

Spectra Industrial supplies equipment and services to PNG's manufacturing, logistics, construction, agricultural, mining, and gas sectors. Our dedicated team delivers specialised support ensuring clients' needs are met on both quality and price.

With offices and warehouses in Lae and Port Moresby, we provide the geographic coverage and local knowledge to support operations anywhere in PNG — when you need it, not when it's convenient.
